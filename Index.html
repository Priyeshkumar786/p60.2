About Bhopal

Bhopal The City Of Lakes also known as capital of Madhyapradesh the city is full of peace and greenery.

Top Attractions:-

  • Upper lake

The place of peace with between the water sound present in sorroundings, it is also said to be heart of bhopal.

  • Manubhan Tekri

Manubhan Tekri, it is also known as Mahaveer Giri, it's also a hilltop Jain Temple and a popular tourist destination in Bhopal. It is loacated in hilltop of bhopal people can enjoy the view as well as picnic with family and friends.

  • Raja Bhoj Statue Lake View

Raja Bhoj statue is located in between the lake at VIP road Bhopal. It is also said to be "Lake View" .

  • Bhojeshwar Temple

Bhojeshwar temple is located at Bhojpur. It is loacated about 25 km from bhopal city. This temple was made by Raja Bhoj and it is famous for the incomplete Bhoje≈õvar temple dedicated to Shiva.

  • Lakes Of Bhopal

  1. Bhojtal (Bada Talaab/ Upper Lake)
  2. Chhota Talaab (Lower Lake)
  3. Shahpura Lake
  4. Motia Talaab (at Taj-Ul-Masajid, Shahjehanbad)
  5. Nawab Siddique Hasan Khan Talaab (First Step lake Below Motia Talaab)
  6. Munshi Hussain Khan Talaab (Second Step lake Below Motia Talaab)
  7. Lendiya Talaab (Ram Nagar Colony)
  8. Sarangpani Lake (Piplani, BHEL area)
  9. Manit Lotus Lake (Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology)
  10. Jawahar Baal Udhyaan Lake (Ravishankar Nagar, Below Char Imli)
  11. Bordi Kalan (Bhadbhada Road)
  12. Preet Nagar Lake (Vidisha Bypass Road)
  13. Nariyalkheda Golf Course Lake (SCEPTA Golf Course)
  14. Laharpur Lake
  15. Mullah Sarovar (Barkatullah University, Habibganj)
  16. Nevri Talaab (Ajgar Talav near Sanjeev Nagar)
  17. New Jail Pond (Ayodhya Bypass Road)
  18. Bairagarh Visarjan Ghat (Sant Hirdaram Nagar)
